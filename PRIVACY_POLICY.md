# Privacy Policy for Elbi GradeSim

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

## Overview

Elbi GradeSim is a browser extension that helps UPLB students calculate their GWA (General Weighted Average) and simulate grades needed for Latin honors. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Access
- **Grades Data**: The extension accesses your grades information from the UPLB AMIS (Academic Management Information System) portal when you are logged in.

### What We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect any personal identification information
- We do NOT collect your AMIS login credentials
- We do NOT track your browsing activity
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All your grades data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`)
- **No External Servers**: Your data is NEVER sent to any external servers
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data is not synced across devices

## Data Usage

Your grades data is used exclusively for:
1. Calculating your current GWA
2. Displaying your grades history
3. Simulating grades needed for target Latin honors
4. Tracking remaining courses in your curriculum

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | To save your grades data locally on your device |
| `host_permissions` for amis.uplb.edu.ph | To access the AMIS portal and read your grades |

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
1. Right-clicking the extension icon
2. Selecting "Remove from Chrome"
3. Or going to Chrome Settings > Extensions > Elbi GradeSim > Remove

Alternatively, clear the extension's data via:
Chrome Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear Browsing Data > Cookies and other site data

## Security

- The extension only works on the official UPLB AMIS website (amis.uplb.edu.ph)
- We use your existing AMIS session - we never ask for or store your password
- All data processing happens locally in your browser

## Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for university students and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
