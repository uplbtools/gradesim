How to use Elbi GradeSim

EXCL.
Exclude from GWA

Toggle a subject out of your GWA calculation. This is useful for NSTP, HK, or other non-bearing subjects. Grades like INC and DRP are handled automatically.

SCHOLAR
Scholar Status

Automatically displayed per semester when your GWA qualifies (≤1.45 for University Scholar, ≤1.75 for College Scholar) assuming you meet unit requirements.

WHAT IF?
Target Latin Honors

In the "What If?" tab, select a target Latin Honor (e.g. Cum Laude). The app will calculate the required average grade you need for your remaining units to achieve it.

EXPORT JSON
Backup & Restore

Use Export JSON to save your grades and settings to a file. Use Import JSON to restore them later or on another device.

WRAPPED
Elbi GradeSim Wrapped

Generate a fun, interactive summary of your academic journey. You can even export the final slide for your Instagram Story!

Terms of Use & Privacy Notice

About This Tool

Elbi GradeSim is an independently developed student web extension designed specifically for UP Los Baños students for GWA tracking and simulation. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the University of the Philippines (UP) System, UPLB, or the UPLB AMIS portal.

1. Privacy & Data

Your academic data stays on your device. We collect, transmit, and track absolutely nothing. No analytics or external servers are used.

2. Saved On Your Device

All grade data is saved only inside your browser. Uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser's data will permanently remove your stored grades.

3. Accuracy Disclaimer

All GWA calculations and target simulations are unofficial estimates. They may not account for incomplete (INC), dropped (DRP), or deferred grades. Always verify official records with the UPLB Office of the University Registrar (OUR).
